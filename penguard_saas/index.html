We don't just flag mistakes. We explain them.

Every correction comes with the grammar rule behind it, so the same mistake gets easier to catch on your own next time.

Original
She don't like apples.
Correction
She doesn't like apples.
Use "doesn't" because "she" is a third-person singular subject — singular subjects pair with "doesn't," not "don't."

Frequently asked questions

How accurate is PenGuard? ▾
PenGuard is built on LanguageTool's linguistic analysis engine, which checks grammar, spelling, and punctuation using rule-based and statistical models trained across billions of sentences. It catches the vast majority of common errors, though like any automated checker it works best as a second pair of eyes rather than a replacement for careful proofreading.
Is PenGuard free?▾
Yes. PenGuard's grammar, spelling, and punctuation checker is free with no account, no per-check word limits, and no credit card required.
Does PenGuard store my text?▾
No. Your text is sent directly from your browser to the checking engine for analysis and is not saved on any server or linked to your identity afterward.
Can I upload documents?▾
You can paste up to 10,000 characters directly into the editor today. Direct .docx and .pdf uploads are on our roadmap.
Which languages are supported?▾
PenGuard currently supports English, with regional variants for US, UK, Australian, and Canadian spelling and grammar conventions.
